JournalFilter.com provides doctors with a tool to keep up-to-date in an age of information overflow and deterioration of information as it drifts away from its source.
JournalFilter.com provides easy access to the original source of research data.
The programme is still under development. Currently, only new research in Cardiology is covered.
Abstract data is kindly provided by the U.S. National Library of Medicine.
